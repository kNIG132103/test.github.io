HR TAG & CHARACTER ENTITES

HR tag is used to draw horizontal line and Various Attributes of HR tag is are:
  1. Size
  2. Width
  3. Color
  4. No shade
  5. Align

hr tag with color=green width=50% align=left size=5px

hr tag with width=75% align=right size=10px noshade

hr tag with color=red width=80% align=center size=12px
